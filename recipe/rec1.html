Chicken Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250g chicken breast
  • 200g pasta
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot and cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add diced onions and minced garlic. Saut√© until onions are translucent.
  3. Add chicken breast to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. Reduce heat to low and add diced tomatoes. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour in the cream and stir well. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  6. Add cooked pasta to the skillet and toss until well coated.
  7. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
